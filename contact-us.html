What’s the best way to apply at MoneyGram?

You can view our open positions and apply on our careers portal.

What is the best contact method?

A MoneyGram recruiter will contact you to start the interview process. The details of the interview will vary depending on what position you apply for. If you are selected for the position, you will receive offer details and your recruiter will help you with the hiring process.

Where are remote positions available?

Remote positions are available from wherever you are, just look for any position marked “Remote” while searching for careers.

Do I have to have a background at a Fintech company to work for MoneyGram?

Position requirements will be listed in the job posting. We welcome individuals of all backgrounds and experience that meets the role requirements to apply at MoneyGram.

Can I apply to more than one job?

Yes. We encourage you to apply to any role that fits your skills and experience.

I forgot my username or password. Do I have to create a new account to apply?

No. You can retrieve your username or password by entering you email and selecting the appropriate option. (**We need to verify with HRIS team on application flow/process)

I am a MoneyGram customer; do I need a separate account to apply for jobs?

Yes. You will need to create a candidate profile on our career page to view and apply for jobs.

How can I tell if my application was completed and submitted?

You will receive a system notification when you complete an application. You can also view the jobs you have applied to and their status in your candidate profile. (**Can we get some info from HRIS to better direct this response?)
